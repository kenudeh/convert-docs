title: Verifiaction
---

While you can start selling airtime immediately on Convert, unverified accounts are limited to a lifetime maximum transaction amount of ₦5,000. To verify your account and increase your transaction limits, follow these steps:
<br></br>

1. Obtain a government-issued color photo ID card (e.g. driver’s license, voter’s card, or passport)<br></br>

2. Get a recent utility bill (not older than three months)<br></br>

3. Take clear photos of both the ID card and utility bill<br></br>

4. Click the **Profile** icon on the navigation menu or at the top-right of your screen, and select **Verification** from the displayed list<br></br>

5. On the new screen, upload both images in their designated fields<br></br>

6. Click the **Submit** button at the bottom of the page
<br></br>

<Tip>
You will get a feedback on your application within 24 hours. Please, note that our accounts team may contact you for additional information regarding your documents.
</Tip>
