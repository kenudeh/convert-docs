### Payments
<AccordionGroup>
    <Accordion title="What if I don't receive my payment?">
        Contact our support team if you do not receive your payout after 24 hours
    </Accordion>


    <Accordion title='Can I change my payment information?'>
        Yes, you can update your banking information at any time. However, we only send payments to bank accounts that matches a user's personal information on file
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title='How soon will I receive my payment?'>
        Once we confirm your airtime transfer, you should receive your payment within five minutes
    </Accordion>

    <Accordion title='How will I get paid after selling my airtime?'>
        Your proceeds will be sent to your bank account on file, after deducting our service charge
    </Accordion>


</AccordionGroup>
