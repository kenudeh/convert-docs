---
title: Banking
---

To transfer fund from your Convert wallet to local bank account, update your banking information as follows:


1. [Log in](https://convert.com.ng/login) or [Sign up](https://convert.com.ng/signup) for an account to access your dashboard 

2. Click the **Profile** icon at the top right of your screen or on the main navigation menu

3. Select **Banking Profile** from the displayed list


4. On the **Banking Profile** page, fill up the displayed form

<Info>
For crime prevention purposes, your bank account name must match the name on your profile.
</Info>

5. Submit the form by clicking the **Submit** button


<Tip>
To update your banking information at any time, click the pencil icon at the buttom of the card displayed on the **Banking Profile** page.
</Tip>
